# 🌍 Global Renewable Energy Asset Intelligence System (GREAIS)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Global Renewable Energy Asset Intelligence System (GREAIS) is an end-to-end Business Intelligence and Data Analytics project designed to monitor, analyze, and optimize renewable energy plant operations across multiple countries.

The project combines SQL, Excel, and Power BI to provide actionable insights into:

- Energy Production
- Revenue Performance
- Plant Efficiency
- Predictive Maintenance
- Sensor Monitoring
- Carbon Credit Generation
- Grid Failure Analysis
- Operational Risk Management

This solution helps stakeholders identify high-risk plants, reduce downtime, maximize profitability, and improve overall operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor renewable energy production performance.
- Track plant efficiency and downtime.
- Analyze revenue and profitability.
- Detect sensor anomalies before equipment failure.
- Reduce maintenance costs through predictive analytics.
- Evaluate carbon credit generation performance.
- Identify failure patterns and operational risks.

## 📂 Dataset Information

The project utilizes 8 interconnected datasets.

### 1. plant_master
Contains plant information.

### 2. energy_generation
Tracks power generation and downtime.

### 3. sensor_readings
Contains temperature, vibration, voltage, and humidity readings.

### 4. maintenance_logs
Maintenance history and costs.

### 5. energy_sales
Revenue and energy sales data.

### 6. weather_data
Weather conditions affecting production.

### 7. carbon_credits
Carbon credit generation and pricing.

### 8. grid_failures
Power grid outage and failure information.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Performance
- Generated over $25 Billion in total revenue.
- Consistent monthly revenue trend with stable growth.

### Plant Efficiency
- Average plant efficiency reached approximately 75%.
- Several plants consistently outperform operational benchmarks.

### Downtime Impact
- Over 30 Million downtime minutes recorded.
- Downtime directly impacts revenue generation and profitability.

### Predictive Maintenance
- Sensor vibration anomalies strongly correlate with equipment failures.
- Early maintenance intervention can significantly reduce outages.

### Carbon Credit Opportunity
- More than 151 Million carbon credits generated.
- Additional monetization opportunities available through carbon markets.

### Grid Reliability
- Grid instability remains one of the major operational risks.
- Failure distribution indicates recurring outage patterns.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Predictive Maintenance Program
Implement machine learning-based maintenance alerts using vibration and temperature data.

### 2. Downtime Reduction Strategy
Prioritize maintenance scheduling for high-risk plants.

### 3. Operational Benchmarking
Replicate best-performing plant strategies across low-performing facilities.

### 4. Grid Risk Mitigation
Introduce backup storage systems and redundancy mechanisms.

### 5. Carbon Credit Optimization
Expand participation in carbon credit trading programs.
